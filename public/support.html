Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a tournament?
Fill out the tournament scheduling form on this page with all required details, and our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your tournament.
What information do I need to provide?
You'll need to provide tournament name, game title, expected date and time, duration, prize pool, format, and your contact information.
How far in advance should I schedule?
We recommend scheduling at least 7-10 days in advance to allow for proper promotion and participant registration.
What games are supported?
We support all major esports titles including Valorant, COD Mobile, FIFA, Clash Royale, Fortnite, PUBG Mobile, and many others.
Is there a fee for tournament scheduling?
Basic tournament scheduling is free. Premium features like enhanced promotion, dedicated support, and custom branding may have associated fees.
